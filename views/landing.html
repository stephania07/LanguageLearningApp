Language learning



This is an application that teaches Tigrigna language for kids between 4 - 5 years old. It covers the basic words that makes it a first step to be familiar with the language and have little conversation with their parents. This app works in a way;

  • Register: get register to create a user account
  • Login: login to your account to see your progress
  • Vocabulary: get introduced with basic words
  • Greetings: study daily greetings
  • Cards: memorize what you learned
  • Quiz: evaluate how far you studied
    Future plan

  • Add higher levels accordingly
